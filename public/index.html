DISCOTHELLO



Explore the depths of strategy in this captivating game where every move can change the tide!



Discover a new dimension of classical gaming with Othello, where the classic strategy game comes to life with 3D graphics. Perfect for beginners and veterans alike.

Choose between single-player mode to improve your skills, multiplayer mode to take on friends and opponents from all over the world, or Player vs AI mode to test your strategies against computer-controlled adversaries. Each game is a new strategic adventure.

Access detailed tutorials and chat with other players via our integrated chat. Share your strategies, learn from others and benefit from a passionate and active community.

About DiscOthello

DiscOthello was inspired by the fact that there aren't many Othello games available in 3D and online too. The project started as a way to challenge ourselves and create something engaging and fun for all ages. It evolved into a portfolio project for Holberton School, showcasing our skills and creativity.


The project was developed over a two-month period, from initial concept to final implementation. We're proud of the work we've done and can't wait to share it with the world.
